Yellowstone National Park, USA

Yellowstone National Park, USA, is a stunning destination known for its geothermal wonders like Old Faithful, hot springs, and geysers. The park also features diverse wildlife, including grizzly bears, wolves, and bison, amidst a breathtaking landscape of mountains, forests, and rivers.

Costa Rica

Costa Rica is a tropical paradise with lush rainforests, exotic wildlife, and stunning beaches. Adventure seekers can surf, zipline, and hike, while nature lovers can explore Arenal Volcano, Monteverde Cloud Forest, and Corcovado National Park. Relax in Tamarindo, Puerto Viejo, or Nosara, and experience Pura Vida (pure life)!
